RIFT

"A portal has opened. Not everyone will survive."

In 3025, a mysterious rift tears open in deep space. You have been commissioned to design the official theatrical movie poster. It must feel vast, dangerous, and cinematic — a world on the edge of destruction.

Mood & Tone
  • Dark & atmospheric
  • Cold cosmos
  • Glowing energy
  • Sense of scale
Design Direction
  • Deep blues & purples
  • Metallic / geometric fonts
  • Dramatic lighting
  • Silhouettes & figures

✦ Canva vs Photopea — Key Difference
  • Canva saves your work automatically to your account in the cloud — you do not need to manually save like in Photopea.
  • However, you should still download a copy at key points in case of any issues.
03
Step 03 — Canvas & Background
SET UP & PLACE BACKGROUND
1

Create a New Design

On the Canva homepage click Create a design → search for A4 → select A4 Document (Portrait). This gives you the correct movie poster ratio. Name your design galactic_rift_poster by clicking the title at the top.

2

Upload and Place Your Background

Click Uploads in the left panel → Upload files → select your space background image. Once uploaded, click it to add it to the canvas. Drag the corners to fill the entire page. Right-click → Set as background to lock it in place behind everything else.

Watch — Setting Up Canva and Placing Your Background
~20–30 sec · CanvaClip3_step3_setup.mp4

Download a copy now! File → Download → PNG or PDF. Save it to your Year 8 Computing folder as a backup.

✦ Can't Fill the Canvas?
  • Click the image → drag any corner handle outward to resize
  • Or click the image → click Edit image → use the crop handles to fill the frame
  • Right-click → Set as background is the quickest method
